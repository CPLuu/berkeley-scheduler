Consider sharing Berkeley Scheduler

We built Berkeley Scheduler because we felt that the alternatives available to Cal Bears had much to be desired. We know this because while building it, we spent a lot of time asking our fellow bears what they wished for from their class schedule planners.

And to this day, we continue to maintain Berkeley Scheduler thanks to your support and encouragement. If you enjoy Berkeley Scheduler, please consider sharing it with your friends so that they too can benefit from it.

Also, if something is not working right, or if you would really like Berkeley Scheduler to have some feature, give us feedback by clicking here, or by clicking the link at the bottom of any page.
